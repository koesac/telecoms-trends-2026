1980s
1G: The Analog Era

Voice Only

The dawn of mobile communication. Analog signals carried the first wireless voice calls, freeing humanity from the tether of the landline.

1990s
2G: The Digital Shift

Text & Data

Digital encryption introduced SMS text messaging. The world discovered it could communicate without speaking a word.

2000s
3G: The Mobile Web

Internet Anywhere

Broadband speeds brought the internet to our pockets. Email, basic web browsing, and the first apps began to reshape society.

2010s
4G: The Streaming Age

Video & Speed

High-speed IP networks enabled HD video streaming, ride-sharing, and the app economy. The smartphone became the center of life.

2020s
5G: The IoT Explosion

Everything Connected

Massive machine-type communication. Smart cities, autonomous logistics, and ultra-low latency gaming became reality.

2030s
6G: The Intelligence Era

Sensing & Holography

Networks that think. Terahertz frequencies merge the physical and digital worlds, enabling immersive holography and sensory internet.
